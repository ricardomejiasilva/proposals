No Online Booking

Discovery flights and first lessons need to be bookable online. When a prospective student is excited at 10pm on a Sunday, they shouldn't hit a dead end — they should be able to lock in a date.

Pilot candidates research costs obsessively. If they can't find training estimates, discovery flight prices, or aircraft hourly rates on your site, they'll find them on a competitor's — and may not come back.

No Testimonials or Social Proof

Happy students exist. Their stories aren't being told. Testimonials from local pilots who trained at KZPH are among the most effective trust signals you can show a prospective student.
