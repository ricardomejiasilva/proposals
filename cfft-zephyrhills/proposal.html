Proposed Services

1. Full Website Rebuild

Modern, mobile-first redesign with complete training program pages (PPL, IFR, Commercial), fleet showcase with photos and specs, instructor bios, pricing estimates, and contact forms throughout. Built on a CMS you can update yourself.

Est. value: $7,000–$9,000

2. Discovery Flight Booking System

Online scheduling with date/time picker, deposit collection, automated email confirmations, and lead capture. Converts web traffic to booked flights 24/7. Every booking captures name, email, and phone for follow-up.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

3. Zephyrhills / Pasco County SEO

Targeted pages for "flight school Zephyrhills," "learn to fly KZPH," "flight training Pasco County," "private pilot Dade City," "discovery flight Zephyrhills." With multiple schools at KZPH, the one that shows up first on Google gets the call.

Est. value: $3,000–$4,000

4. Fleet + Instructor Showcase

Full aircraft profiles with make, model, avionics, and photos. Instructor bios with backgrounds, ratings, and teaching approach. Students who can visualize their training before they call are much more likely to follow through.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

5. Pricing Transparency Page

Training program estimates, aircraft hourly rates, discovery flight pricing. Students who see pricing upfront are more likely to follow through, and they show up with realistic expectations.

Est. value: $1,000–$1,500

6. Testimonials + Google Reviews Automation

Student success stories and automated review requests after first solos and checkride passes. Google Reviews improve your local ranking, which brings in students who've never heard of CFFT but are searching in your area.

Est. value: $1,000–$1,500
