Proposed Services

1. Full Website Rebuild

Modern, mobile-first redesign with complete training program pages (PPL, IFR, Commercial), fleet showcase with photos and specs, instructor bios, pricing estimates, and contact forms throughout. Built on a CMS you can update yourself.

Est. value: $7,000–$9,000

2. Discovery Flight Booking System

Online scheduling with date/time picker, deposit collection, automated email confirmations, and lead capture. Converts web traffic to booked flights 24/7. Every booking captures name, email, and phone for follow-up.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

3. Zephyrhills / Pasco County SEO

8 targeted pages targeting "flight school Zephyrhills," "learn to fly KZPH," "flight training Pasco County," "private pilot Dade City," "discovery flight Zephyrhills," "PPL training near Tampa north." With multiple schools in KZPH, SEO is the tiebreaker — the school that shows up first gets the call.

Est. value: $3,000–$4,000

4. Fleet + Instructor Showcase

Full aircraft profiles (make, model, avionics, photos) and instructor bios with backgrounds, ratings, and teaching philosophy. Students who see exactly what they'll fly and who they'll fly with are significantly more likely to book.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

5. Pricing Transparency Page

Training program estimates, aircraft hourly rates, discovery flight pricing, total PPL cost estimates with national comparisons. Students who get pricing upfront convert at higher rates and arrive with realistic expectations.

Est. value: $1,000–$1,500

6. Testimonials + Google Reviews Automation

Student success stories and automated post-milestone review requests (first solo, checkride pass). Google Reviews directly improve your Business Profile ranking — which generates organic discovery flight bookings from students who've never heard of CFFT before.

Est. value: $1,000–$1,500
