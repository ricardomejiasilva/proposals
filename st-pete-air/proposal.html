Executive Summary

St. Pete Air trains pilots at one of the most compelling general aviation airports in Florida — Albert Whitted, a downtown St. Petersburg waterfront facility with views of the Sunshine Skyway and Tampa Bay from the traffic pattern. That's a genuine competitive advantage. It's almost entirely absent from your website.

I'm a full-stack developer and SaaS founder based in St. Pete (literally a 5-minute drive from Albert Whitted), working on my Private Pilot License. I'm proposing a straightforward trade: I build you a modern digital presence worth $15,000–$20,000, and in exchange, you let me train for my PPL while I pay your variable costs.

The Exchange

Proposed Services

🌅

1. Location-Forward Website Redesign

Complete rebuild centered on Albert Whitted's unique advantages. Hero section with aerial photography/video of Tampa Bay from the pattern. "Fly over the Sunshine Skyway on your first lesson" as the lead message. Modern, mobile-optimized, fast-loading. Built on a CMS you can update yourself.

Estimated value: $6,000–$8,000
🗓️

2. Discovery Flight Booking System

Integrated scheduling on the homepage and training pages. Prospects pick a date, time, and optionally pay a deposit to confirm. Automated confirmation emails with what to bring, where to park, what to expect. Captures name, email, phone for follow-up.

Estimated value: $2,500–$3,500
🧮

3. Interactive Training Cost Calculator

An interactive tool where prospects input their goals and current experience level and get a personalized cost estimate for PPL, instrument, or other ratings. Replaces the "call us for pricing" dead-end with a trust-building conversion tool.

Estimated value: $1,500–$2,000
🔍

4. SEO Content Strategy

6 targeted blog posts/landing pages covering "flight school St. Petersburg FL," "learn to fly Albert Whitted," "downtown St. Pete flight training," "DA40 rental Tampa Bay," and related terms. Structured for Google ranking, written to convert readers into leads.

Estimated value: $2,000–$3,000
✈️

5. Aircraft Rental Portal

A dedicated rental section with hourly rates, aircraft specs and photos, checkout requirements, rental agreement summary, availability request form. Targeted toward certified pilots in the Tampa Bay area who need a rental — a distinct customer from student pilots.

Estimated value: $1,500–$2,500
⭐

6. Automated Google Review System

After key milestones (first solo, checkride pass, rental checkout), the system automatically sends a personalized follow-up email asking the student or pilot for a Google Review. More reviews → better local rankings → more inbound leads without additional effort.

Estimated value: $1,000–$1,500
