Proposed Services

1. International Student Landing Page

A full dedicated page: housing options with photos, what the training experience looks like, visa guidance, FAQs for international students, testimonials. SEO-targeted for "flight school Florida with housing," "PPL training USA international students," "pilot training Florida accommodation."

Est. value: $3,500–$4,500

2. Advanced Training Showcase Pages

Dedicated pages for aerobatics, tailwheel, and spin/upset recovery, each with descriptions, prerequisites, what to expect, and pricing. SEO-targeted for "aerobatics training Florida," "tailwheel training Venice," "spin training Sarasota." The pilots searching for these offerings are motivated and will pay for them.

Est. value: $2,500–$3,500

3. Online Booking System

Discovery flight and inquiry scheduling with separate flows for local vs. international prospects. The international flow captures housing interest and travel planning context up front, so you know what you're dealing with before you call them back.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

4. Pricing Transparency + Location Marketing

Pricing pages for all major training offerings. Plus a visual "why Venice" section, the island, the low-traffic airspace, the beach proximity. International students choosing between US flight schools are also choosing where they'll live for months. Make that argument explicitly.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

5. SEO Content Strategy

6 posts targeting local and international search terms: "flight school Venice FL," "aerobatics training Florida," "flight school Florida housing for students," "tailwheel endorsement Sarasota." Written to rank in search and actually convert readers into inquiries.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

Revenue Potential

International students who travel for training often stay for the full pipeline, PPL through commercial. Even 2–3 per year from a proper landing page could be worth $50k–$100k+ in additional annual revenue. Aerobatics and tailwheel students tend to be enthusiasts who pay without negotiating and refer their friends. The SEO work keeps paying after it's done.
