Proposed Services

1. Embedded Calendar Booking

Replace the form-based "schedule a consultation" with an embedded calendar — prospects pick an actual date and time, get instant confirmation. The form creates uncertainty; a calendar creates commitment. Higher conversion on your existing traffic.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

2. Financing Calculator

Interactive calculator: program selection → down payment → loan term → estimated monthly payment + links to financing partners. For a $12k–$65k investment, financing is often the deciding factor. Make it easy to calculate yes.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

3. Central Florida SEO Content

8 targeted posts: "flight school Lakeland FL," "career pilot program Tampa Bay," "zero-to-hero pilot Central Florida," "PPL cost Lakeland 2026," "commercial pilot training Florida." These are searches happening right now from your target students.

Est. value: $2,500–$3,500

4. Instructor Bios + Fleet Details

Staff page with CFI photos, credentials, career path, and teaching approach. Fleet page with aircraft specs and avionics. For $55k+ programs, students are choosing a training relationship — they need to vet the instructors and equipment.

Est. value: $2,000–$2,500

5. AI Prospect Assistant + Student Portal

Chat widget for 24/7 prospect Q&A + a lightweight student portal for active students (hours tracking, upcoming lessons, documents). Both tools differentiate Dobbs at your price point and convert the cohort that researches for weeks before calling.

Est. value: $3,000–$4,000

Revenue Impact

You're already the most professional independent in the area — these upgrades are about conversion efficiency, not brand building. Estimated: 2–3 additional students per year captured from the "almost booked" and "found via Google" cohorts. At $12k–$65k per student (depending on program), that's meaningful incremental revenue from work done once.
