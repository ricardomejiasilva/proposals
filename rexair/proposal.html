Proposed Services

1. International Student Landing Page + Funnel

A dedicated page for international prospects: M-1 visa process explained, US Dept of State recognition front and center, Purdue Global degree pathway, accelerated ATP timeline, FAA certification details. SEO-targeted for "FAA pilot training Florida international," "flight school M-1 visa USA," "foreign pilot training certificate USA."

Est. value: $4,000–$5,000

2. Cirrus Owner Marketing Hub

Dedicated Cirrus training pages: transition training, CSIP recurrency, CAPS training, Gen 6/7 aircraft specifics. Platinum status featured prominently. Targets "Cirrus training Naples FL," "Cirrus transition instructor Florida," "Cirrus SR20 SR22 training Southwest Florida." These are the highest-paying training clients in general aviation.

Est. value: $3,500–$4,500

3. AI Lead Nurturing System

An interactive qualifier on the site: career vs. recreational, budget range, timeline, domestic vs. international. Routes to the right program, collects email, enters leads into the right follow-up sequence. Answers questions at any hour. The Purdue Global pathway involves a complex, multi-touch decision process. AI handles the early qualification so your team isn't doing it manually.

Est. value: $5,000–$7,000

4. Online Booking System

Cirrus intro flights, discovery flights, and accelerated program consultations bookable online. Deposit collection, automated confirmations, CRM integration. Cirrus owners, career-path pilots, and international students don't want to exchange voicemails to get started.

Est. value: $2,500–$3,500

5. SEO Content Strategy

8 targeted posts covering: Cirrus training in Southwest Florida, accelerated ATP programs in Florida, international pilot training on an M-1 visa, the Purdue Global aviation degree path, 529 plans for pilot training, Naples vs. Sebring training, and the Rexair Accelerated Pilot Path. Each post targets a specific audience who has reasons to choose Rexair, if they can find it.

Est. value: $3,000–$4,000

6. 529 Plan Content Expansion

Turn the existing 529 page into a full content strategy: FAQ page, blog post for parents researching aviation careers for their kids, training cost calculator, dedicated landing page. Almost nobody in flight training markets this. It should be a Rexair brand position, not a single page nobody finds.

Est. value: $2,000–$2,500
