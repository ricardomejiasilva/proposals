Proposed Services

1. Pricing Transparency (Programs Page Upgrade)

A clear pricing section with ranges for PPL, instrument, and commercial training, plus an interactive cost calculator. This is probably the highest-impact single change on the whole site, most people won't call without a rough number first.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

2. North Tampa SEO Content Strategy

6 posts targeting your actual service geography: Lutz, Wesley Chapel, New Tampa, Odessa, Land O'Lakes. Organic traffic from people in your area who are already searching. You just need to be the answer that shows up.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

3. AI Lead Capture Chatbot

24/7 chat widget that answers pricing, scheduling, and "what aircraft do you fly?" questions. Captures name and email for leads who aren't ready to book yet. Works alongside FlightCircle, doesn't replace it.

Est. value: $2,000–$2,500

4. Instructor Bio Pages

A staff page with photos, credentials, flight hours, and teaching philosophy for each CFI. Students research their future instructor before committing. Give them something to find.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

5. Google Review Automation + Email Nurture

Automated milestone review requests after first solos and checkrides. Plus a 5-email sequence for cold leads who visited but didn't book, so slow deciders hear from you over the next few weeks instead of disappearing.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

Revenue Impact

Each of these upgrades targets a specific place where prospects drop off. Pricing alone typically moves contact form conversion by a noticeable amount, so you'd see more students from your existing traffic before any SEO kicks in. Add the north Tampa SEO content and you're reaching people who can't find you at all right now. A conservative estimate: 3–4 additional PPL students per year, which works out to $36k–$68k in annual revenue.
