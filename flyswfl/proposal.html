Proposed Services

1. Owner-Aircraft Instruction Landing Page

A dedicated, SEO-optimized page for instruction in owner-supplied aircraft: BFRs, IPCs, aircraft transitions, complex/high-performance endorsements, tailwheel (if applicable). Targets "CFI for my aircraft Fort Myers," "flight instructor BFR SWFL," "aircraft transition training Cape Coral." This is your differentiator — let's actually market it.

Est. value: $3,000–$4,000

2. Online Booking System

Dual booking flows: student pilots schedule discovery flights and lessons; owner-aircraft pilots specify their aircraft type and what they need. Every lead captured automatically — no more losing a BFR client because they couldn't find your phone number.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

3. Pricing Transparency Pages

Hourly instruction rates, BFR/IPC packages, discovery flight pricing, safety pilot rates. Pilots comparing CFIs choose the one who gives them the information upfront. Transparency is a competitive advantage when your competitors don't offer it.

Est. value: $1,000–$1,500

4. Fort Myers / SWFL SEO Campaign

6 targeted posts: "flight school Fort Myers," "CFI Page Field KFMY," "BFR Fort Myers," "discover flying SWFL," "flight instructor Cape Coral," "private pilot training Fort Myers." Own the local search results before a bigger school does.

Est. value: $2,500–$3,500

5. Owner-Pilot Content Hub

3 foundational posts: "What to expect in your Biennial Flight Review," "How to find a CFI for your own aircraft," "Complex endorsement checklist." Written to rank for the exact searches your ideal owner-aircraft clients are making — and to position Fly SWFL as the expert on the subject.

Est. value: $2,000–$2,500

6. Testimonials + Google Reviews Automation

Testimonials section from students and owner-aircraft clients, plus automated Google Review requests after BFRs, checkrides, and lesson completions. Reviews are the #1 trust signal for pilots searching for a CFI — and they compound indefinitely.
