Proposed Services

1. Full Website Rebuild

2. Discovery Flight Online Booking

Online scheduling for your $129.99 discovery flights. Date picker, deposit collection, confirmation email. Works when you're not. Captures name, email, phone automatically.

3. Maintenance Scheduling Portal

Aircraft owners submit their make, model, and squawks online, get an acknowledgment, and schedule drop-off. Right now your Cessna Service Center status could be pulling in maintenance customers you're not seeing, but only if people can find and contact you online.

5. Award & Credential Showcase

A dedicated section for the 3x Airport of the Year award, Cessna certification, repair station status, and on-site Examiner. Right now these are buried. They should be the first thing a prospect sees.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

After a first solo, checkride pass, or maintenance job, an automated email goes out asking for a Google Review. More reviews improve your Google ranking. It compounds over time without any manual effort.
