Proposed Services

1. Mission-Forward Homepage Redesign

A rebuilt homepage that leads with "Aviation with purpose" — your Proyecto Global partnership, anti-trafficking work, faith-based approach. For the students who would specifically choose Posada for these reasons, this landing page becomes the deciding factor.

Est. value: $4,000–$5,000

2. AI Chatbot: "Talk to Nick"

A chat widget trained on your FAQ — pricing questions, training timeline, aircraft fleet, mission background — active 24/7. Captures contact info. Routes serious leads with context to you directly. Personal, helpful, always available.

Est. value: $2,500–$3,500

3. Discovery Flight Booking System

Booking CTA on every training page — date picker, confirmation, lead capture. A motivated visitor doesn't need to cold-call; they book and you get a warm lead with name, email, and stated interest.

Est. value: $2,000–$2,500

4. Pricing Calculator

Interactive training cost estimator. Student inputs current hours and training goals; gets realistic cost range. Kills the "I don't know if I can afford this" objection before it's raised. Dramatically improves call-to-inquiry conversion.

Est. value: $1,500–$2,000

5. SEO Content Strategy (Local + Mission)

6 posts targeting "flight school Zephyrhills," "faith-based pilot training Florida," "learn to fly Tampa Bay," "Christian aviation school," "missionary pilot training." Capture the audience most likely to become long-term students and advocates.

Est. value: $2,000–$3,000

6. Google Review Automation

Automated post-milestone review requests after first solo, checkride pass, maintenance completion. Systematic review volume builds Google ranking over time — reaching more students in your area organically.

Est. value: $1,000–$1,500

Revenue Projection

Conservative: 3–4 extra PPL students per year from improved SEO + booking. At $12k–$17k per student, that's $36k–$68k in annual incremental revenue from work done once. Additionally, the mission-forward landing page and faith-aligned SEO targets a cohort of students who may not be comparing price but specifically seeking your mission alignment.
